Types of fencing

EPEE

There are two types of grip, the Belgian and the French, and the tip that connects to the blade is the nozzle only, but the parts that are allowed to hit the opponent are the whole body.

FOIL

There are two types of its grip, the Belgian and the French, and the tip that connects to the blade is the nozzle only, but the parts that are allowed to hit the opponent are the chest only. This type is characterized by its light weight.

SAPER

There is only one type of fist with it, and the end that connects to the blade is the entire front part, but the parts that are allowed to hit the opponent are the chest and face only.
